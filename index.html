Our Program

Built on
Discipline & Sound

The Washington High School Marching Band has been a cornerstone of the Bay Area music community since 1891. Our program pushes students to master their instruments, perform under pressure, and grow into disciplined, collaborative musicians.

From Friday night football games to regional competitions, the Huskies compete at the highest level while fostering a culture of mentorship, respect, and artistic excellence.
